About WSR

The Weekly Strategic Roundtable (WSR) is a research-oriented discussion forum focusing on defence, security and strategic studies. Launched on 12 August 2024, WSR brings together students, researchers and practitioners to examine contemporary geopolitical developments through an academic lens.

This website hosts our first E-Magazine, featuring curated articles, session reports and reflections emerging from our weekly deliberations.
