About WSR

The Weekly Strategic Roundtable (WSR) is an academic initiative dedicated to structured discussions on defence studies, strategy, and international relations. Started on 24 August 2024, WSR brings together undergraduate, postgraduate and research scholars to critically analyse contemporary security challenges.

This e-magazine serves as a curated outlet for the best ideas emerging from WSR sessions – including research papers, policy briefs, and reflective essays.

Submission Guidelines

We welcome original, unpublished work related to defence, strategic studies, geopolitics, and security issues.

  • Word limit: 2,000–4,000 for articles; 800–1,200 for commentaries.
  • Follow a consistent citation style (e.g., Chicago or APA).
  • Attach your brief bio (2–3 lines) with institutional affiliation.
  • Send submissions as a Word document (.docx).
