About WSR

The Weekly Strategic Roundtable (WSR) is a research-oriented discussion forum focusing on defence, security and strategic studies. WSR aims to bring together students, researchers and practitioners to examine contemporary geopolitical developments through an academic lens.

Through weekly sessions and curated issues, WSR encourages analytical writing, evidence-based arguments and informed debate on themes affecting India and the wider world.

What this e-magazine hosts:

  • Curated articles emerging from WSR discussions
  • Session reports and brief analytical notes
  • Commentaries on current defence and geopolitical developments
  • Special issues on selected strategic themes
